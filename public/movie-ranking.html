Daniel's Favorite Movies of All Time

Here are the three top ones that come to mind at the moment.

(Please check back frequently for updates.)

A Beautiful Mind

This movie spoke to me because it was the story of how one can decide to be well, even when one has an incurable illness. I also love (spoiler alert) the way the movie tricked me, and the reveal that made me want to watch the whole movie over again. This will be a theme in all of my favorite movies.

Arrival

This movie stars one of the most versatile actors I know, Amy Adams. Everything she does turns to gold. It houses a great surprise reveal, and the story is beautiful and moving.

Source Code

This movie makes you say, "Whaaaaa?" right from the start. And then, as the situation slowly reveals itself, you recognize one of the most cleverly thought out science-fiction ideas that there is.
